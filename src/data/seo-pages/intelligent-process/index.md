## FAQs About Intelligent Process Automation

### What is intelligent process automation in simple terms?

It’s how you use AI and bots to automate tasks usually done by people—saving time and reducing mistakes.

### How is IPA different from regular automation?

IPA uses AI and machine learning to make decisions, while basic automation follows fixed rules.

### Do I need to be tech-savvy to use these tools?

No. Tools like UiPath and Power Automate offer low-code or no-code builders, and Binary Ideas provides full training.

### Is IPA safe for sensitive business data?

Yes, especially with platforms like IBM Cloud Pak that offer enterprise-grade security.

### How soon can I see results?

Most clients report ROI within 3–6 months, depending on the processes automated.

### What industries benefit most?

Finance, healthcare, retail, logistics, and legal services see huge gains—but IPA is valuable for nearly every business.
