## 10 Daily Tasks You Can Automate in Under 10 Minutes

### 1. Email Filtering and Smart Replies

AI tools filter emails by topic or importance and can draft replies with natural language processing.

### 2. Calendar Scheduling and Reminders

Calendly or Google Calendar AI integrations eliminate the back-and-forth of booking appointments.

### 3. Social Media Posting and Monitoring

Buffer or Hootsuite plus AI schedules posts and suggests the best publish times.

### 4. Data Entry and Organization

AI extracts, cleans, and organizes data from multiple sources—no more manual typing.

### 5. Online Customer Support

Chatbots handle basic inquiries 24/7 and escalate only when needed.

### 6. Invoice Generation and Expense Tracking

Tools like QuickBooks auto-generate invoices and flag upcoming due payments.

### 7. File Management and Backup

Zapier or Google Drive automations sort, rename, and back up files automatically.

### 8. Task Prioritization and To-Do Lists

Apps such as Motion or Todoist prioritize your day based on urgency and habits.

### 9. Content Creation and Summarization

Jasper AI or Notion AI drafts blog posts, newsletters, and meeting summaries.

### 10. Personal Habit Tracking

AI nudges help you drink more water, read more books, or maintain other habits.
