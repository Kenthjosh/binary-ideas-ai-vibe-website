## What is AI Automation in Ecommerce?

AI automation combines artificial intelligence with automation tools to transform how online stores operate and serve customers. Unlike rule-only systems, AI learns from data and shopper behavior to make smarter, real-time decisions.

Key technologies include:

- <span class="text-[#2b7fff] font-semibold">Machine Learning</span> – Finds patterns and improves decisions over time.
- <span class="text-[#2b7fff] font-semibold">Natural Language Processing</span> – Powers 24/7 chatbots and voice assistants.
- <span class="text-[#2b7fff] font-semibold">Computer Vision</span> – Enables visual search and product recognition.
- <span class="text-[#2b7fff] font-semibold">Predictive Analytics</span> – Forecasts demand and optimizes pricing/inventory.
- <span class="text-[#2b7fff] font-semibold">Generative AI</span> – Creates SEO-ready product copy and personalized content.

Together, they automate marketing, supply chain tasks, and customer service—delivering tailored experiences at scale.
