## What Does It Mean to Automate Workflows with AI?

At its core, workflow automation with AI means using smart software to manage repetitive business processes. Unlike traditional automation (think: email autoresponders), AI automation learns, adapts, and personalizes.

- <span class="text-[#2b7fff] font-semibold">Traditional automation:</span> Works on rules you set (if X happens, do Y).
- <span class="text-[#2b7fff] font-semibold">AI automation:</span> Uses data, context, and patterns to make decisions in real time.

Instead of manually following up with every lead, an AI agent can score, segment, and send personalized replies based on behavior. Instead of scheduling back-and-forth emails, an AI system can book meetings directly into calendars.

For businesses juggling multiple tools, AI acts as the glue—integrating platforms, cutting down errors, and ensuring nothing slips through the cracks.
